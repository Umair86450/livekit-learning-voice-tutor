Thinking Portfolio

Part 0 is about how to teach humans to thrive in the future. The rest of the book is how to build the future.

After completing all ten chapters, you assemble your Thinking Portfolio -- a documented record of cognitive growth across question formulation, error detection, systems thinking, first principles reasoning, communication, AI collaboration, ethical reasoning, creation, decision-making, and meta-learning. You then repeat the identical baseline assessment to produce a quantified before-and-after comparison of your thinking skills.

What You Will Do​

Build a Growth Map quantifying your strongest improvement and biggest remaining gap
