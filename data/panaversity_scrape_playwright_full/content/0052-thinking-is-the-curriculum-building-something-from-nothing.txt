Chapter 8: Building Something From Nothing

AI is the most powerful remixing engine ever built. It cannot originate. The student who can create something that did not exist before has the one skill AI cannot replicate.

Core Skill: Creation and Originality

Creation is not summarization, reorganization, or polishing. It is the act of producing something new -- an argument nobody has made, a solution nobody has tried, a connection nobody has seen. This chapter puts you through the process of creating under constraints, with AI available as an assistant but not as the creator.

What You Will Learn​
How to produce original solutions under time pressure with zero external assistance
How to collaborate with AI on creative work while tracking exactly who contributed what
How to measure your own creative value by testing whether AI could independently produce your output
How to evolve a solution through three visible drafts: unassisted, AI-collaborated, and reflection-refined
How to use the Creation Log and Three-Draft Evolution as reusable workflows for all future creative work
